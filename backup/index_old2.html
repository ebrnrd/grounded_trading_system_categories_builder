How to use

Files tab — Load your trade_categories.ltx, then add as many .ltx config and .xml string files as you need. Use "Clear All" to remove all files of one type at once.

Items tab — Browse all known item IDs with their real in-game names. Hover any item for its full description. Filter by prefix chips or placement status. Drag items directly into sections.

Sections — Click a section name directly to rename it inline. Inheritance entries are shown as a separate list under the header — drag to reorder, click ✕ to remove, type to add new ones. Drag items from the left panel or between sections to reorganize.

Export — Click Refresh Export then Download or Copy.
